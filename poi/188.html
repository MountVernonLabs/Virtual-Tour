• The Miller's House

    Today's gift shop is a reconstruction of Washington's hired miller's home. Washington had three millers between 1771 and 1799. His best miller, William Roberts, was also his worst. Washington noted that Roberts was "an excellent miller,…equally a good Millwright, and Cooper" but "when drunk so great a madman," which is what led to his dismissal in 1785. Washington rehired Roberts in 1799, but illness forced him to leave Mount Vernon within two month’s time.
