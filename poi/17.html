• Story: Dining with the Washingtons

    "Dinner was served up about ½ after three…The President came into the portico about ½ an hour before 3 and talked freely upon common topics with the family. At dinner he placed me at the left hand of Mrs. Washington; Miss [Nelly] Custis sat at her right and himself next to her about the middle of the table. There was very little conversation at dinner. A few jokes passed between the President and young la Fayette [sic], whom he treats more as his Child than as a Guest…."—Benjamin Henry Latrobe, July 17, 1796
