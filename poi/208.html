• George Washington Medals (1 of 2)

    The medals date from 1778 to 1932 and commemorate Washington’s accomplishments and his legacy. The medals mark landmark moments in Washington’s military career and important dates in the nation’s history.
  • Washington Before Boston, 1776, Struck 1785, Bronze (2 of 2)

    The United States Congress commissioned this medal to commemorate the British evacuation of Boston, one of George Washington’s earliest victories in the American Revolution. On the reverse, Washington is depicted overlooking the city on horseback with his aides at Dorchester Heights, preparing to besiege the city in the event that the British did not retreat.
