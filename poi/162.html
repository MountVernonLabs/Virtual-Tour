• Jacques Necker (1 of 2)

    George Washington received this porcelain bust of French finance minister, Jacques Necker, in 1790, as a gift from Charles Hector, Comte d'Estaing, commander of the first French fleet to assist the Americans during the Revolution. Necker had played a key role in France's financial contributions to the American Revolution. An engraved silver plaque below the bust celebrates Necker as "Qui Nobis Restituit Rem [He who put our affairs in order]."

    Gift of the Historical Society of Pennsylvania, 1969 [W-2548]
