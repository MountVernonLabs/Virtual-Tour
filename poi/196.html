• Benjamin Franklin (1 of 3)

    The bust shows Franklin in 1785, when he was 79 years old. Franklin, the most well-known American before the American Revolution, returned to Pennsylvania in triumph in that year after his extraordinary success as a diplomat to the French court.
  • Benjamin Franklin (2 of 3)

    This painting of Franklin was done in Europe in 1785 and reflects the image of the man as he was leaving the post as the first American ambassador to France in which he secured the American-French treaty during the American Revolutionary war, secured millions in loans to pay for the war, and helped negotiate the treaty of Paris.
  • Benjamin Franklin (3 of 3)

    This bust of Benjamin Franklin was created in 2013 by StudioEIS, based upon a painting of that year and the bust of Franklin done by Jean-Antoine Houdon in 1777 while Franklin was securing French support for the American Revolution.
