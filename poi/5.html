• Campus is always buzzing with activity, and McCain Student Center is the hub of The College of Idaho’s social scene. McCain Pub and its adjoining patio are popular hangouts and host regular Open Mic nights, visiting bands, karaoke and other student events. Also housed in McCain are a movie theatre, game room, digital production studio, study lounges, the Eros Theatre, the Outdoor Program and student government offices, mail center, College Store, and offices for the College’s student affairs staff. With more than 50 student organizations, it’s easy for you to get involved and make a difference on campus. Whatever your interests, you’ll develop lifelong friendships with your classmates.
