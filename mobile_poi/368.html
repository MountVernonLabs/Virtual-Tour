• John A. Washington II

    John Augustine Washington II was George Washington’s great-nephew and was raised by Bushrod and Julia Ann Blackburn Washington at Mount Vernon. He inherited the estate when his uncle, Bushrod, passed away in 1829. John Augustine II and his wife are both buried in the tomb. Mount Vernon was inherited by their son, John Augustine Washington III. The obelisk in front of the tomb memorializes John Augustine II and his family’s role as owners of Mount Vernon.
