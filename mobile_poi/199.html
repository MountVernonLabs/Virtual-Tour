• John Adams (1 of 3)

    The bust shows Adams in 1785, when he was 50 years old. By this time he had already been an important national figure for 10 years, and had helped negotiate the Treaty of Paris which assured American Independence. In this year he would meet King George III as the first American ambassador to England.
