  • Bushrod Washington Family Papers, 1662-1835 (2 of 2)

    Bushrod Washington (1762-1829), was the son of John Augustine and Hannah (Bushrod) Washington, nephew of President George Washington, and an associate justice of the U. S. Supreme Court. His collection, the Bushrod Washington Family Papers, 1662-1835, is comprised of correspondence and legal documents related to several branches of the Washington family.
