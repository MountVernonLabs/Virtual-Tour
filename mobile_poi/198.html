• Alexander Hamilton (1 of 3)

    This bust of Alexander Hamilton depicts Hamilton as he would have looked in 1785, at 30 years old, when he was building his connections and legal practice in New York City.
  • Alexander Hamilton (2 of 3)

    Hamilton had served as an aid-de-camp to George Washington and in the Continental Congress by 1785. In this year he played an important role negotiating boundary lines between New York and Connecticut as a lawyer representing New York State.
  • Alexander Hamilton (3 of 3)

    This bust of Alexander Hamilton was created in 2013 by StudioEIS. Hamilton is often considered the most handsome of the Founders, and this bust carries on that perspective. He would really become bust-worthy for his role in encouraging the ratification of the Constitution and his masterful tenure as the first Secretary of the Treasury.
