• Cornerstone

    Lawrence Washington, George Washington’s older half-brother, owned Mount Vernon from 1743 until his death in 1752. A sandstone tablet carved with the initials “LW” may be a marker of changes that he made to the house, although its exact date and purpose remain a mystery. In order to preserve the fragile stone, the original tablet was removed (it is now on display in the Donald W. Reynolds Museum), and it has been replaced by an exact copy.
